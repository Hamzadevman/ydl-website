Who We Are

Forging the voices of tomorrow

YDL is a premier Model United Nations club dedicated to empowering young minds with the art of diplomacy, negotiation, and leadership. We believe every great leader starts by learning to listen.

Our conferences bring together passionate delegates to tackle the world's most pressing challenges — from climate policy to global security — in a structured, professional, and intellectually stimulating environment.
