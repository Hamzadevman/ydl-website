Who We Are

Forging the voices of tomorrow

YDL — Young Debaters & Leaders — is a club dedicated to empowering young minds with the art of diplomacy, negotiation, and leadership. We believe every great leader starts by learning to listen.

Our events challenge delegates to build nations, defend policies, and navigate the complex web of international relations — in a structured, professional, and intellectually demanding environment.

The Format

Design a nation. Claim your seat.

At YDL Spring Event 2026, participants don't represent existing countries — they invent one. Every delegate designs a fictional nation with its own policies, economy, political system, strengths, and challenges.

During the event, countries will debate pressing global topics, negotiate strategic alliances, and compete to prove their nation's worth on the world stage.

Only the most compelling, well-argued, and diplomatically savvy nations will earn their seat at the table. This is not simulation — this is statesmanship.
